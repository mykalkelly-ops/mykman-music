Songs Listened

There isn't a perfect list of every song Mykal has ever heard.

This app can reliably track songs he liked enough to save into the monthly playlists, and it can often infer full albums he listened to from those playlist picks.

But sometimes Mykal listens to an album, likes nothing, and never keeps any track from it in Apple Music. That kind of listening disappears into the void, and rebuilding every one of those moments by hand would be way too much work.

So this number is best understood as: the songs we can reasonably account for from liked tracks plus albums we know he listened to, not a perfect cosmic record of every single listen.
